IT Support FAQ

Answers to the most common IT questions. For anything else, open a ticket in the IT portal or visit the helpdesk on floor 2.

How do I reset my password?

Use the self-service portal at reset.acme.example. If your account is locked, call the helpdesk at extension 4357. Password resets require MFA confirmation on your registered device.

How do I set up the VPN?

Install the GlobalConnect client from the software center, sign in with your corporate account and approve the MFA prompt. The VPN is required for all remote access to internal systems.

When do I get a new laptop?

Laptops are refreshed every three years. You will receive an automatic notification from asset management when your device is eligible. Damaged devices are replaced immediately via an IT ticket.

Can guests use the office Wi-Fi?

Yes. Visitors can use the ACME-GUEST network; reception issues day passes. The guest network has no access to internal systems.

How do I request new software?

Submit a software request in the IT portal. Licensed software requires manager approval and a cost centre. Open-source tools from the approved list install without approval.
